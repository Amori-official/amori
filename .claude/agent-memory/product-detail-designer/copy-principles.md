## Brand Voice
Quiet, warm, minimal — "a trusted friend who knows fabrics explaining things." Never salesy, never superlatives like 최고의/완벽한. Use specific, believable claims.

## Product Description (`description` field in mock-data)
- 2–4 sentences in polished, conversational Korean
- Lead with what the product IS made of and the structural reason it works (e.g., "6겹으로 직조")
- Follow with how it FEELS or what it does in daily use (sensory + practical)
- Close with a usage-stage cue (신생아부터, 이유식 시기에도)
- Avoid: "최고", "완벽", "탁월한"
- Use: "부드럽고", "촘촘하고 포근한", "안심하고", "매일의 순간을 조금 더 편안하게"

## Material Field
Multi-paragraph with `\n\n` for Prose breaks (rendered with `whitespace-pre-line`).
Structure: material name + what gauze IS + why 6-layer + specific specs bullet list.
Always include: 소재, 원산지, KC 인증.

## Feature Labels
10–14 characters. Concrete, descriptive. Examples: "6겹 거즈 구조", "면 100% 국내산", "KC 안전 인증", "넉넉한 프리 사이즈", "스냅 단추 고정".

## Feature Body
2 sentences max. Sentence 1: what makes it special. Sentence 2: what that means for the baby/parent.

## Brand Story (`brandStory` field)
2–3 sentences. Philosophical/emotional. Connects product to parent's daily experience. Ends on the reason why quality/safety matters (피부에 가장 먼저 닿는 것이니까요 등).

## Care Instructions
Bullet list format with `·` prefix. Order: 세탁온도 → 단독/분리 → 세탁방법 → 건조기 여부 → 건조방법 → 직사광선 → 첫 세탁 주의.

## Size Guide
State: free size range (신생아 ~ 24개월) → exact cm dimensions → neck circumference range → practical usage note per age stage.
